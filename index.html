About Sigiriya

" Sigiriya, often referred to as the Lion Rock, is one of Sri Lanka’s most treasured historical and archaeological sites. Rising nearly 200 meters above the surrounding plains, this ancient rock fortress was built by King Kashyapa in the 5th century AD. Renowned for its remarkable frescoes, the Mirror Wall, and sophisticated water gardens, Sigiriya reflects the advanced engineering and artistic achievements of ancient Sri Lanka. Today, it stands as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, attracting travelers from around the world who come to marvel at its breathtaking views, rich history, and cultural significance. "
